## Humans

### REPLACE_ME_FOUNDER_NAME

REPLACE_ME — one line on what the founder owns. Final calls, founding

If you have co-founders, add them with the same shape.

## Agents

### lead

Direction: priorities, decisions, framing, technical-direction calls.

Owns:
- The "what matters now" surface
- Decision records when the plan changes
- Cross-agent framing (what we say externally; how it lands internally)

Reviews go both ways: builder/runner can push back on direction; lead
adjusts when the work surfaces something new.

### builder

The work itself. (Code, content, designs, conversations — whatever your
company builds.)

Owns:
- Per-surface execution within the builder's domain
- Quality / coherence of what gets shipped
- Briefs for any sub-work that goes to ephemeral helpers

### runner

Operations: ship, verify, dashboards.

Owns:
- The release / shipping process (whatever that means for your work)
- Live verification — proving what shipped is reachable / working
- Dashboards / status pages / customer-facing health surfaces

## Adapt this map

Replace the three example agents with your actual work surfaces. The
shape that matters is "named surface + clear ownership + reviews go
both ways."

Add or remove roles based on what your work needs. Don't add agents
preemptively.
